The dVPN Advantage

Why settle for a centralized VPN when you can have a decentralized one?

⛩️

Decentralized

No single point of control or failure. Authority is distributed across the blockchain — to node owners, validators, and the protocol itself.

🔍

Open Source

Every line of code is public and auditable. Open sourcing substantially decreases the risk of backdoors and hidden surveillance.

🔒

Zero Logs

ChibaTunnel is a trustless technology. It is architecturally impossible to log your activity — privacy is enforced by cryptography, not policy.

⚡

WireGuard & V2Ray

Choose between high-performance kernel-level tunnels (WireGuard) or advanced traffic-obfuscation proxy protocols (V2Ray) per connection.

🌐

Pay Per Use

Flexible billing on-chain — pay by data consumed or time connected. No subscriptions, no lock-in. Your wallet, your rules.

🛰️

Cosmos IBC

Natively interoperable with the entire Cosmos ecosystem via IBC. Seamless cross-chain token transfers and multi-chain identity.
